About Maybelline

    Maybelline is a well-known and highly recognized makeup brand that has been in 
    existence for over a century. It was founded in 1915 by Thomas Lyle Williams,
    who was inspired to create makeup products for his sister, Mabel. The brand's name
    "Maybelline" is a combination of Mabel's name and the word "Vaseline."

    Maybelline is known for its affordable yet high-quality cosmetics, making it accessible
    to a wide range of consumers. The brand offers an extensive range of makeup products,
    including foundations, concealers, mascaras, eyeshadows, lipsticks, and more. 
    They are constantly innovating and introducing new products that cater to 
    the latest beauty trends.

    One of Maybelline's most iconic products is the Great Lash Mascara, which has become 
    a cult favorite and a staple in many makeup bags. It has garnered a strong following 
    for its ability to deliver voluminous and defined lashes.

    Maybelline is also recognized for its inclusivity, striving to provide makeup options 
    for diverse skin tones and types. They offer a diverse shade range in their foundation
    and concealer lines, ensuring that people with different skin tones can find suitable options.
